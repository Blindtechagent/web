How to play?

Welcome to the Rock Paper Scissors game!

To play, follow these steps:

  1. Choose either Rock, Paper, or Scissors by clicking on the corresponding icon.
  2. The computer will also make a random choice.
  3. Based on the following rules, the winner will be determined:
  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Scissors beats Paper

The game will keep track of your score and the computer's score. The first player to reach 10 points wins the game.

Join Us: We're Hiring!

At Blind Tech Agent, we are committed to making life easier for everyone, especially visually impaired individuals. We're looking for passionate and talented people to join our team. By joining us, you will make a real difference and work in a supportive and innovative environment.

Who We're Looking For:

Web Developers:
Do you have experience building and maintaining websites? Do you know your way around HTML? We need you to help enhance our platform and make it more user-friendly.

Educational Content Providers:
Are you skilled at creating and delivering educational materials? We need your expertise to educate and support our users.

YouTube Video Creators:
Do you enjoy making engaging videos and have at least 50 subscribers on your YouTube channel? Join us to produce content that informs and inspires our audience.

Writers/Promoters:
Do you have a talent for writing and promoting? Help us spread the word about Blind Tech Agent and reach more people.

Why You Should Join Us:

  • Make a Real Impact: Your work will improve the online experience for everyone, especially the visually impaired.

  • Develop Your Skills: Gain new skills and enhance your existing ones through hands-on experience.
  • Expand Your Network: Connect with like-minded individuals and grow your professional network.

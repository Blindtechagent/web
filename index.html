Learn Basic Web Development in 20 Days with Blind Tech Agent

Are you ready to dive into the world of web development?

After the successful completion of our previous course, "Basics of Microsoft Excel," we are excited to introduce our newest offering: a Basic Web Development Course that you can complete in just 20 days. Led by our team blind tech agent, this course will be conducted via Google Meet, making it accessible to all interested learners.

What Will You Learn?

  • Understanding the role of a web browser

  • Exploring what is a website

  • Learning how websites are built

  • Introduction to programming languages used in web development

  • Understanding the components of a web page

  • Overview of search engine optimization (SEO)

  • Differentiating between client-side and server-side development

  • And many other interesting topics related to web development

Requirements:

One of the best parts of this course is that there are no prerequisites or specific requirements to enroll.

All you need is an Android phone to join the virtual classes. Additionally, notes and other helpful materials will be provided to enhance your learning experience.

Class Timing:

The classes will be held from 6:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. This can be changed later as per your schedule, making it convenient for working professionals and students alike to participate and learn at their own pace.

Course Details:

  • The full course is free of cost, making it accessible to everyone interested in learning web development.

  • The last date for registration is June 11, 2024, so be sure to secure your spot before the deadline.

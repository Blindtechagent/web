BTA Media Player

Welcome to the BTA Media Player, a powerful and accessible media player designed to enhance your media playback experience. Whether you're watching videos or listening to audio, our media player offers a range of features and functionality to make your experience enjoyable and user-friendly.

Key Features:

  • Play, pause, rewind, and fast forward your media content with ease.
  • Adjust the playback speed to your preference.
  • Toggle mute.
  • View media information including file name, type, and size.
  • Toggle full-screen mode for an immersive viewing experience.

Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • Press Ctrl + Space to Play/Pause.
  • Use Ctrl + Left Arrow to Rewind 10 seconds.
  • Utilize Ctrl + Right Arrow to Fast Forward 10 seconds.
  • Toggle Mute/Unmute with Ctrl + M.
  • Adjust Playback Speed by pressing Ctrl + S.

If the shortcuts are not working as expected, please try pressing both Control keys (Ctrl) simultaneously along with the respective shortcut key. This should help you control your media playback effectively.

Enjoy your media with the BTA Media Player and experience the power of accessible technology.

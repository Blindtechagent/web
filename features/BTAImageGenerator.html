BTA Image Generator

Welcome to the BTA Image Generator! This tool lets you create images from text descriptions. Just type what you imagine, and we'll turn it into a picture.

How to use the tool:

  • Type a description of the image you want in the box below. For example, "A cat sitting on a tree" or "A spaceship flying in space."
  • Click the "Generate Image" button.
  • Wait a moment, and your image will appear below.

To download the image:

  • On a phone or tablet: long press on the image to see the option to download.
  • On a laptop or computer: press the application key (or right-click) on the image to find the option to download.

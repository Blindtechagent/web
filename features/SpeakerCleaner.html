Mobile Speaker Cleaner

Usage Instructions:

This tool is designed to help you maintain the quality of your speaker.

If your speaker encounters moisture or needs cleaning, follow these steps:

  • Click the "Start Cleaning" button below.
  • A 10-second audio signal will play to help clean the speaker.
  • Adjust the volume to a safe level and keep the speaker away from your ears.
  • The cleaning will automatically stop after 10 seconds.

By using this tool, you can help remove unwanted particles or moisture from your speaker and improve its performance.

Features

Welcome to our Features page! Here, you can find a variety of features to help you with different tasks.


  • Age Calculator

    Calculate your age quickly and easily with our Age Calculator. Simply enter your birthdate and get your age instantly.

  • BTA Image Generator

    Introducing the BTA Image Generator! This tool turns your ideas into pictures. Just describe what you want – like 'a cat sitting on a tree' or 'a spaceship in space' – and we'll create the image for you. It's easy, creative, and lets you bring your imagination to life instantly!

  • Online Calculator

    Need to do some quick calculations? Our Online Calculator is perfect for you. It includes all basic functions such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

  • Currency Converter

    Introducing our Currency Converter tool! Easily convert between different currencies using real-time exchange rates. Just enter the amount, select the currencies, and click 'Convert.' Perfect for travel or international transactions.

  • Games

    Discover our website's games, specially designed for visually impaired individuals. With accessible controls, descriptive narration, and inclusive features, everyone can enjoy an immersive gaming experience. Try it now and embrace the joy of accessible gaming for all.

  • Image to Text Converter

    Image2Text Converter: A tool that turns images into text, helping visually impaired people by extracting the text from the image. It has an easy design and accurate conversion, making it simple to access printed materials and documents, improving accessibility and independence.

  • Link Shortener

    Our Link Shortener is very useful tool: it shortens any URL you have. Just paste your long link, and it will quickly create a shorter, more shareable version for you to use.

  • Media Player

    Discover our user-friendly media player, made for everyone. With special features for accessibility, it's easy for all users to enjoy their favorite music and videos without any hassles.

  • Note Pad

    Looking for an easy and efficient way to capture your ideas and stay organized? Look no further than Note Pad. With its intuitive interface and saving, Note Pad is the ultimate note-taking tool for unlocking your productivity and creativity.

  • Password Generator and Manager

    A random password generator is a useful tool for creating secure passwords that are difficult to crack. It generates a series of random characters, including upper and lower case letters, numbers, and special symbols, making it nearly impossible for others to guess your password. It also allows you to save it.

  • Speaker Cleaner

    This tool is designed to help you maintain the quality of your speaker. By using this tool, you can help remove unwanted particles or moisture from your speaker and improve its performance.

  • Stop Watch

    This stopwatch is a handy time management tool that enables users to measure the duration of any activity, including workouts, cooking, and meetings. With its user-friendly interface and accurate timing capabilities, a stopwatch is an essential tool for anyone looking to keep track of time.

  • Text Analyser

    Looking to get a better handle on your writing? Our text analyzer is the perfect tool for you! With the ability to quickly and accurately count characters, words, and lines, this tool will help you get a clear picture of your text in just seconds. Try it out today!

  • Text to Image Converter

    Introducing the Text to Image Converter! This tool helps you convert written text into image format. Select your preferred font style, color, and background, and your text will be transformed into a visually appealing image, perfect for sharing on social media, blogs, or anywhere else!

  • Text to PDF Converter

    A text to PDF converter is a valuable tool that transforms plain text documents into PDF format. This conversion preserves formatting and ensures compatibility across devices. It simplifies sharing, printing, and archiving of textual content. Useful for creating professional-looking documents from simple text files efficiently and conveniently.

  • Text Encoder and Decoder

    The Text Encoder and Decoder is a handy tool that helps keep your text safe and secure. It works by turning your plain text into a secret code using special techniques. This tool ensures that sensitive information stays private and cannot be easily understood by unauthorized people. It's easy to use and gives you peace of mind knowing your data is protected.

  • ToDo List

    Keep track of your tasks and get things done efficiently with our ToDo List tool. It's simple and easy to use.

  • Telegram Link Generator

    Generate links to share with your friends and contacts using the Telegram Link Generator. It's quick and easy to use, allowing you to create links that open a specific chat with a friend, channel, group, or bot on Telegram.

  • WhatsApp number link generator

    Generate a clickable link to share your WhatsApp number with others. This tool makes it easy for people to contact you directly on WhatsApp.

We hope you find our Features page helpful and useful for your needs. If you have any questions or feedback, feel free to contact us.

Shootout Challenge

Welcome to the Shootout Challenge! In this game, your objective is to shoot enemies before they attack you. Be quick and aim carefully to score points!

Enemies will appear randomly from one of three directions: left, center, or right. You must shoot them before they get the chance to shoot you.

The game will speed up as you score more points, so stay alert and try to reach 10 points before the enemy does. Click "Start Game" to begin and good luck!


How to Play

    • Use Alt + Right Arrow or click the Shoot Right button (which has a gun icon pointing to the right) to fire at enemies on the right.
